Research Summary

Abstract

Overview of the proposed facial emotion recognition system.

Facial Emotion Recognition Using Deep Learning

Human emotion recognition plays a significant role in human-computer interaction, healthcare, education, surveillance, and intelligent systems. This research presents a lightweight Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) integrated with Haar Cascade face detection for real-time facial emotion recognition.

The proposed model was trained using the FER-2013 dataset containing seven emotional classes: Angry, Disgust, Fear, Happy, Sad, Surprise, and Neutral. The trained model was deployed as a Flask web application, allowing users to upload images and receive instant emotion predictions.

The experimental results achieved approximately 60.98% test accuracy, demonstrating that lightweight CNN models can provide effective emotion recognition while maintaining fast inference suitable for real-world applications.
